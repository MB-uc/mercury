# Material events checklist

**Purpose**: Apply this checklist when reviewing a company's own news, stories, press releases, or media section during the situational awareness step. Flag any article that changes the company's portfolio, name, structure, ownership, or leadership.

**When to use**: After fetching the company's news/media section directly (typically `/news`, `/media`, `/our-stories`, `/press-releases`, `/newsroom`, or equivalent). Review the most recent 10–15 articles and run through this checklist before leaving the step.

**Why this step is mandatory**: Search results cannot be relied upon for very recent events due to indexing lag. The most material events often appear on the company's own domain first.

---

## Checklist

| Event type | Check |
|------------|-------|
| Merger, acquisition, or merger of equals announced | □ |
| Major divestiture announced or completed | □ |
| Company name or brand change | □ |
| Listing or headquarter change | □ |
| CEO or CFO appointment or departure | □ |

---

## Required action on any ticked item

If any item is ticked, it **must** appear in the situational awareness section with:

- **Date** of the announcement or event
- **Source URL** (the specific article or press release)
- **Relevance** to the website assessment — what does this mean for the site's content, structure, or messaging?

It may also require updating the scope or synthesis sections of the stage report.

---

## Integration with compliance self-check

Check **I** in the compliance self-check confirms this step was completed:

> **(Stage 1 only)** Company's news/stories/media section was fetched directly in Step 3b — not only searched via web_search. Most recent 10–15 articles reviewed. Any missed material events added to situational awareness before saving.

If the company's news section was not directly fetched, fetch it now and re-run the check before saving the stage artefact.
